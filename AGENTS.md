## Dinner archive

`data/dinners-archive.ts` documents all 20 Brooklyn Suppers dinners (February 2023 – May 2026). Read it before:
- Suggesting or writing about dinner themes (avoid repeating recent cuisines; note what's been done)
- Structuring or writing menu card copy for `data/menus.ts`
- Understanding the host's culinary voice and recurring elements
- Advising on photos (the `photo` field tracks which dinners have images)

Key patterns to know: bitter greens appear throughout as a signature side; honey-fermented kumquats are a house ingredient; Italian and Spanish are the most frequent bases; the typical structure is snacks → shared main → sides → dessert.
